What is GiraffeWare?

GiraffeWare is a generalization of PostCardWare, TeddyBearWare, etc.

In these alternate schemes, the author of some software product asks for some small non-monetary fee. PostCardWare authors ask for post cards, TeddyBearWare authors ask for teddy bears, ... you get the idea.

GiraffeWare is the merging of all of these. You get to send me anything you like! (Well, nothing nasty, of course, but anything you think I might like, you can send my way). The reason it's called GiraffeWare is that I really like giraffes a lot, so if you send me something giraffe-related, I'll be particularly pleased :).
